JUDGEMENT

SIP - Generic Judgment (PRACK )
 [generic_PRACK.txt]
 
 - Request-Line:
     Method: Must be "PRACK". #7.1 the present tense.
 
 - Headers:
   * RAck
     Must Exist. [RFC3262]
     response-num: MUST equal with response-num field in the reliable provisional response. [RFC3262]#4. the present tense.
     CSeq-num: MUST equal with CSeq header's CSeq-num field in the reliable provisional response. [RFC3262]#4. the present tense.
     Method: MUST equal with CSeq header's Method field in the reliable provisional response. [RFC3262]#4. the present tense.
 
   * To
       tag-param: Must exist. [RFC3261-12-35]
 
   * Call-ID
       call-id: Must equal that of the Initial INVITE. [RFC3261-12-40]
 
   * CSeq
       sequence number: Must be the value incremented by one from that of the previous request sent by NUT. [RFC3261-12-42,43]
 
   * Route
     Must include a Route header field. [RFC3261-12-49]
      route-param: Must contain the route set values in order, including all parameters. [RFC3261-12-49][RFC3262-4-6]
 
   * Require
       Must not exist "100rel". [RFC3262-4-1]
 
   * Alert-Info
       Must not exist. [RFC3262]
 
   * Call-Info
       Must not exist. [RFC3262]
 
   * Contact
       Must not exist. [RFC3262]
 
   * Expires
       Must not exist. [RFC3262]
 
   * In-Reply-To
       Must not exist. [RFC3262]
 
   * Organization
       Must not exist. [RFC3262]
 
   * Priority
       Must not exist. [RFC3262]
 
   * Reply-To
       Must not exist. [RFC3262]
 
   * Subject
       Must not exist. [RFC3262]
