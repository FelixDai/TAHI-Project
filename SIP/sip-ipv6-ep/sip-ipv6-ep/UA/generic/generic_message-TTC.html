JUDGEMENT

SIP - Generic Judgment (message TTC)
 [generic_message-TTC.txt]
 
 Generic judgement items for SIP message (be base on TTC)
 
 - Headers:
   The length of each header(including CRLF) must be 255 bytes or less. [JJ-90.24-13.2.2]
 
   * Via
     via-branch: The length(excluding 'z9hG4bK') must be less than or equal to 32 bytes. [JJ-90.24-13.2.2]
 
   * From
     tag-param: Must be less than or equal to 32 bytes. [JJ-90.24-13.2.2]
 
   * To
     tag-param: Must be less than or equal to 32 bytes. [JJ-90.24-13.2.2]
 
   * Call-ID
     callid: Must be less than or equal to 64 bytes. [JJ-90.24-13.2.2]
 
   * Contact
     Must be less than or equal to 64 bytes. [JJ-90.24-13.2.2]
     user: Must be less than or equal to 32 bytes. [JJ-90.24-13.2.2]
