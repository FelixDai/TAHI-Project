JUDGEMENT

SIP - Generic Judgment (SDP TTC)
 [generic_SDP-TTC.txt]
 
 Generic judgement items for SDP (be base on TTC)
 
   * "o=" line   o=<username> <session id> <version> <network type> <address type> <address>
       <username>: Must be less than or equal to 10 bytes. [JJ-90.24-13.2.2]
       <session id>: The first session id must be less than or equal to 999900. [JJ-90.24-13.2.2]
       <version>: The first version must be less than or equal to 999900. [JJ-90.24-13.2.2]
 
   * "s=" line  s=<session name>
       <session name>: Must be less than or equal to 10 bytes. [JJ-90.24-13.2.2]
 
   * "m=" line  m=<media> <port> <transport> <fmt list>
       <media>: Must be "audio". [JJ-90.24-1.2]
       <fmt list>: Must include "0"(G.711u-Law). [JJ-90.24-10.1]
 
   * "a=" line  a=rtpmap:<payload type> <encoding name>/<clock rate>[/<encoding parameters>]
       <payload type>: The payload type that encoding name is G711u-Law must be "0". [JJ-90.24-10.2.1]
